Help & Support

Most Frequently Asked Questions

Food-Related Allergies

For information on allergens, contact the restaurant and make sure it suits your dietary needs. You can find the possibility to contact the restaurant as follows:
  • You can find the restaurant’s contact information from the restaurant’s info page.
  • Contact the restaurant for ingredients and food preparation.
Please leave notes for the chef with instructions (no nuts, no lactose, no gluten, etc.). The ability to leave notes for the chef are in the cart preview or before adding a specific item to the cart.

Delivery didn’t arrives ?

Common reasons for delayed deliveries are rush in the restaurant or rush with Ciaoo Food delivery. We automatically adjust our time estimates based on actual times.

Some items missing or wrong in order ?

Contact our support in-app chat and we'll fix this. Sometimes restaurants or couriers make mistakes. We do whatever we can to fix every human error.

Payment

Ciaoo Wallet

Ciaoo Food Balance is a system that records potential refunds. Upon refund, the amount will be added as a credit, which you will be able to use for the next order.
You can verify your current balance in the Payments section . If you did not find it there, it is possible that it is null or your application is an older version, we recommend reinstalling it.
You will also see your balance in the order when ordering. If the amount is positive, the payment of the order will be reduced by the amount of credit.

Price calculation

All items available for ordering in the application are individually priced. Before confirming your order, you can check your cart and view the sum of the selected items.
The price for delivery is then added based on your location.
Note: Prices displayed in the application may differ from prices in the restaurant. Please note that the rates are determined by the restaurant.
